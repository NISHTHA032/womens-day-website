About International Women's Day

International Women’s Day is celebrated on March 8 every year. It honors the achievements of women in social, economic, cultural, and political fields.

Inspirational Women

Marie Curie

First woman to win a Nobel Prize and pioneer in radioactivity research.

Malala Yousafzai

Youngest Nobel Prize winner and activist for girls' education.

Kalpana Chawla

First Indian-born woman astronaut in space.
